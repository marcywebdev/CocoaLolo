About Us

Know More About Cocoa Lolo!

Cocoa Lolo specializes in hand-crafted, unique crocheted items, jewelry, sculptures, artwork, and crafts. CocoaLolo has managed to bring smiles to many faces with particularly geeky, heartwarming, adorable, items made with an admirably quality. Beginning as just a local business in a small suburb outside of Los Angeles, CocoaLolo was founded to put an official brand name to all of the products our artist has been creating since 1992.
